How it works?

1.Calculate the preliminary cost of your device.

To do this, click “Add” and select the device model you want to return. You can bring multiple devices –this way your benefit will be even greater, because
their costs add up.

2.Select the device model you want to buy from the list.

You will receive a preliminary cost estimate taking into account the benefits.

3.Complete an online application for Trade-in.

After you submit your application, you will see a QR code with a link to the diagnostic application. Download it in advance - this is will help save time on
installing it in the store and speed up the transaction. All further actions in the application will be carried out by our manager in the store. After completing the
online application, our specialist will contact you. He will tell about Trade-in conditions, required documents and the assessment process. It will also reserve a
future purchase for you in the store. You can refuse the call - then all the necessary information will be sent to you by email.

4.Submit your device for instant diagnostics at any iSpace store.

The device must be in working order (turn on), have no significant damage to the case and screen, as well as traces contact with liquid. If the device meets
these requirements, we will conduct diagnostics to determine its final cost. It will take no more than 5 minutes. When assessing, we take into account chips,
damage and check documents.

5.Use the estimated cost when purchasing a new device.

All of your trade-in devices can be collectively used to pay for your purchase. What would you like It’s up to you to purchase, there are no restrictions on the
assortment. The remaining amount can be paid by card, cash, or apply for installments or credit.
